About us

Crafted 4 Wellness is a company dedicated to deliver top quality products to improve our customers life. C4W owns and operates premium sports nutrition, health and beauty products. At the moment C4W will be launching a new image and start exploring the CBD industry. More and new products will be released on the coming months. Together, all these products will provide our customers around the world with a comprehensive line of powdered, bar, tablet, capsule and ready to drink products. Company Owned & Operated Manufacturing With state-of-the-art production facilities in Fort Collins, Colorado and our corporate offices in LAs Vegas, NV C4W is one of the few companies to manufacture in every product category. From the beginning in 2018, Crafted 4 Wellness has always taken a hands-on approach to maintaining the very highest standards of quality.

Uncompromising Quality


Before a single machine is turned on in our manufacturing facilities, C4W procurement professionals carefully select the most premium raw materials. A Certificate of Analysis is required for every ingredient, which is then tested and retested for compliance. Quality assurance performs daily inspections of our current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMPs) compliant facilities.
